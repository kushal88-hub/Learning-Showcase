Mr. Pratap Purna

Mr. Pratap Purna is from Dagana Dzongkhag and completed his Post Graduate Diploma in Education (PgDE) from Samtse College of Education. He has been in the teaching service for 3 years and represents the Social Science Domain. He is also a facilitator for SEN education.

Video Summary

In his video reflection, Mr. Pratap Purna discusses his experiences in the immersion program, highlighting the value of roadmap planning for students and how practical internships help learners develop skills, knowledge, and values during their vacations.
