Mr. Tshering Tobgay

Mr. Tshering Tobgay has his roots in the capital city of Thimphu. He graduated from the Paro College of Education and has more than fifteen years of teaching experience. He is an active member of the English Domain in the school and also serves as a facilitator for SEN education.

Video Summary

In his video reflection, Mr. Tshering Tobgay shares insights from the immersion program, and touches on two takeaways, roadmaps and internship explaining how roadmap gives the learners the ownership of learning and how internship provides skills, values and knowledge and make fruitful use of their vacation.
